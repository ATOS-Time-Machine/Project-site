Problem Definition

The process for managing overtime could be made less ‘Administration’ intensive given the right tool. Ideally any application used for overtime would be linked into ESS and either produces an approved Special Claims form which can be submitted to HR or ties into payroll etc once approved by a Team Leader etc. This would give a ‘one stop shop’ for engineers and managers so produce and manage overtime requests through to submission.
